General

Fluxkode offers a comprehensive suite of web services: Web Development, Web Design, Web Applications, HTML Email Templates, Figma to Code conversion, and WordPress Development (including WooCommerce). We handle projects from concept to launch, offering both design-first and code-first approaches depending on your needs.

We are based in Mumbai, India, but we work with clients globally. We've delivered projects for businesses in the US, UK, Europe, UAE, Australia, and across Asia. Our team is comfortable working across time zones, and we use tools like Slack, Notion, and Loom to stay in sync with clients regardless of location.

Fluxkode was founded in 2018. Over 6+ years, we've grown from a two-person team to a 12-member agency, completing 150+ projects for clients across 20+ countries. Our longevity in the industry is a testament to our quality and the trust our clients place in us.

We love working with both! For startups, we understand budget constraints and offer phased delivery options to help you launch lean and scale fast. For established businesses, we bring enterprise-level thinking and can handle complex, large-scale projects. Our pricing and process are flexible to suit both.

Process & Timeline

Our process has four phases: Discovery (understanding your goals and creating a roadmap), Design (wireframes and high-fidelity mockups for your approval), Development (coding with regular progress updates), and Launch (thorough testing, deployment, and post-launch support). You're involved at every stage.

Timelines vary by project scope. A landing page or simple brochure site typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full multi-page website takes 3–5 weeks. Complex web applications can take 8–16 weeks or more depending on features. We always provide a detailed timeline estimate before the project begins, and we stick to it.

Our standard packages include 2–3 rounds of revisions at each stage (design and development). We find this is usually more than enough when clients are involved during the discovery phase. Additional revision rounds can be added to any project for a nominal fee.

Ideally, you provide the copy and brand assets (logo, images). However, we work with trusted copywriters and photographers who can handle content creation as an add-on service. We'll always use professional placeholder content during the design phase so you can see exactly how the final product will look.

Pricing & Payment

Our pricing starts from ₹25,000 for landing pages and goes up depending on complexity. Full websites typically range from ₹60,000–₹1,50,000. Web applications are priced on a custom basis. We provide a detailed, transparent quote after an initial consultation — no hidden fees, ever. Visit our Services page for rough pricing tiers.

We use a milestone-based payment structure: 50% upfront to kick off the project, 25% upon design approval, and 25% on final delivery. For larger enterprise projects, we offer monthly billing options. We accept bank transfers, UPI, PayPal, and Stripe.
